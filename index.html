How to Cook at Cooking Cookies

Cookies are a common bake, but if done right they can be simply exquisite.
They are a common dessert for special occasions and the holidays, and a amazing dessert for you to make.

Ingredients:

8 Tablespoons of Salted Butter
1/2 Cup White Sugar
1/4 Cup Packed Light Brown Sugar
1 Teaspoon Vanilla
1 Egg
1 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
1/4 Tablespoon Salt (but feel free to add some extra if you want)
3/4 Cup Chocolate Chips

The Steps:

Step 1: Melt your butter fully in the microwave then let it cool down for a minute.
Step 2: Beat Butters and Sugars until fluffy and creamy
